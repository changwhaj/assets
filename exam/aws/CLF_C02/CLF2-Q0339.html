C. AWS IAM Identity Center (AWS Single Sign-On) AWS Single Sign-On (SSO) enables centralized authentication and authorization across multiple AWS accounts and other business applications. It allows users to sign in once and access resources in various accounts without the need for separate credentials for each account. This helps simplify access management in a multi-account environment.
