B. Blue/green with canary. The blue/green with canary deployment allows the company to route a small percentage of traffic to the new version of the application, allowing the company to test the new version before routing all traffic to it. The canary deployment allows the company to gradually roll out the new version and test it with a small subset of live traffic, and after the canary period, the company can route all the remaining live traffic to the new version. This option meets the company's requirements for initially exposing only 10% of live traffic to the new version, and then immediately routing all remaining live traffic to the new version.
