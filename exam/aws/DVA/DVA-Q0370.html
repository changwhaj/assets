D D. Immutable deployment is the deployment option that will meet the company's requirements of avoiding any downtime. In an immutable deployment, Elastic Beanstalk creates a new Auto Scaling group and deploys the new version of the application to the new group. Once the new group is ready, traffic is routed to the new group and the old group is terminated. This approach ensures that there is no downtime during the deployment, as the old group continues to serve traffic until the new group is ready.
