CloudFront: is a content delivery network (CDN) service from AWS, designed to distribute content globally at high speeds by caching content in edge locations close to users. This is the most cost-effective way to serve dynamic content globally, which is exactly what the scenario requires. ALB as the origin: By setting the existing ALB as the origin for CloudFront, the CDN will fetch content from the web application running on the EC2 instances behind the ALB, effectively caching and delivering content from geographically closer locations to users
