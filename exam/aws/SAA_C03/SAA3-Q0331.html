° 15 Mbps bandwidth with 70% max utilization limits the effective bandwidth to 10.5 Mbps or 1.31 MB/s. ° 20 TB of data at 1.31 MB/s would take approximately 193 days to transfer over the network. ° This far exceeds the 30 day requirement. ° AWS Snowball provides a physical storage device that can be shipped to the data center. Up to 80 TB can be loaded onto a Snowball device and shipped back to AWS. This allows the 20 TB of data to be transferred much faster by shipping rather than over the limited network bandwidth. ° Snowball uses tamper-resistant enclosures and 256-bit encryption to keep the data secure during transit. ° The data can be imported into Amazon S3 or Amazon Glacier once the Snowball is received by AWS.
