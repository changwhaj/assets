Amazon FSx also provides features such as automatic backups, data encryption, and native multi-Availability Zone (AZ) deployments for high availability. It can be easily integrated with other AWS services, such as Amazon S3, Amazon EFS, and AWS Backup, for additional functionality and backup options.
