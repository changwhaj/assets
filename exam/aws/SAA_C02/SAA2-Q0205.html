Which solution should a solutions architect recommend?

No, there are automated snapshots for RDS available https://aws.amazon.com/rds/faqs/ Why do I have more automated DB snapshots than the number of days in the retention period for my DB instance? It is normal to have 1 or 2 more automated DB snapshots than the number of days in your retention period. One extra automated snapshot is retained to ensure the ability to perform a point in time restore to any time during the retention period. For example, if your backup window is set to 1 day, you will require 2 automated snapshots to support restores to any within previous 24 hours. You may also see an additional automated snapshot as a new automated snapshot is always created before the oldest automated snapshot is deleted.
