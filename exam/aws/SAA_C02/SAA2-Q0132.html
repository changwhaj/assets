I/O performance for video processing, 300 TB of very durable storage for storing media content, and 900 TB of storage to meet requirements for archival media that is not in use anymore.

  • A. Amazon Elastic Block Store (Amazon EBS) for maximum performance, Amazon S3 for durable data storage, and Amazon S3 Glacier for archival storage Most Voted

  • D. Amazon EC2 instance store for maximum performance, Amazon S3 for durable data storage, and Amazon S3 Glacier for archival storage Most Voted
