Option B: While it is true that DNSSEC uses zone-signing keys (ZSKs) in addition to KSKs, AWS Key Management Service (KMS) is not involved in creating ZSKs for DNSSEC in Route 53. Option C: Delegation Signer (DS) records are used to establish a chain of trust from a parent zone to a child zone, not between subdomains within a zone.
