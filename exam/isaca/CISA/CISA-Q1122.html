Selected Answer: A
The primary role of an IT steering committee is to provide oversight and guidance on major IT projects and resource allocation. It ensures that IT initiatives align with the organization's strategic objectives and priorities. While other activities such as approving IT security awareness training content, assigning IT services to infrastructure components, and developing IT policies and procedures may be important, overseeing major projects and resource allocation is the key responsibility that directly supports the board in fulfilling its IT governance duties.
