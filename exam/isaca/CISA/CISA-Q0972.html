Selected Answer: А
Understanding the purpose of each spreadsheet is crucial for prioritizing them in an end-user computing audit. This involves gaining insight into the significance of the spreadsheet in the financial reporting process, the data it handles, and its overall impact on financial reporting. By prioritizing based on the criticality and importance of each spreadsheet, the IS auditor can focus efforts on auditing the most significant and high-risk spreadsheets, ensuring that potential risks and control issues are appropriately addressed.

Understanding the purpose of each spreadsheet Understanding the purpose of each spreadsheet is crucial because it allows the auditor to assess the relevance and significance of each spreadsheet in the financial reporting process.
