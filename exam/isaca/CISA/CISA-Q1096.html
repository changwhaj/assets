Selected Answer: D
The most important stakeholder to involve in the review of processes that prevent fraud within a business expense claim system is the Business process owner. The business process owner is responsible for the end-to-end management of the business process, and in this case, the owner of the expense claim system. They have a comprehensive understanding of the process, including its objectives, controls, and potential areas of vulnerability to fraud. The business process owner is directly responsible for the system under review and is best positioned to provide in-depth knowledge and context for the audit.

Business Process Ownership: The business process owner is responsible for the day-to-day operations and performance of the expense claim system. Their involvement ensures that they take ownership of implementing any recommended changes or improvements to prevent fraud effectively.
