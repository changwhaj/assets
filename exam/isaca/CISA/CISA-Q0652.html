Selected Answer: A
Including incident classification criteria within an incident response plan provides more visibility to the impact of disruptions by categorizing incidents based on their severity, which helps prioritize response and recovery efforts. This ensures that incidents are handled in a timely and efficient manner, minimizing their impact and allowing for a more effective response. Having more visibility into the impact of disruptions enables organizations to make informed decisions and allocate resources appropriately, which is key to effective incident management.
