Of the options provided, the most appropriate answer is A, "approve the IT strategy." The board of directors has the responsibility of approving the IT strategy and ensuring that it aligns with the overall goals and objectives of the organization. This is a critical step in ensuring that IT initiatives are aligned with business objectives and that the organization's resources are effectively allocated to support these objectives.
