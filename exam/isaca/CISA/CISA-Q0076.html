An IS auditor is following up on prior period items and finds management did not address an audit finding. Which of the following should be the IS auditor's NEXT course of action?

  • A. Note the exception in a new report as the item was not addressed by management.
  • B. Interview management to determine why the finding was not addressed.
  • C. Recommend alternative solutions to address the repeat finding.
  • D. Conduct a risk assessment of the repeat finding.

B. Interview management to determine why the finding was not addressed. When an IS auditor follows up on prior period items and finds that management did not address an audit finding, the next course of action should be to interview management to determine the reasons for not addressing the finding (Option B). Understanding the underlying causes and challenges can provide insights into any obstacles that may have prevented the resolution of the issue. This information can guide the auditor in formulating appropriate recommendations or alternative solutions and in documenting the situation accurately.
