Selected Answer: A
Recovery Point Objectives (RPOs) define the maximum acceptable amount of data loss in the event of a disruptive incident. By aligning backups with RPOs, an organization ensures that the backup process captures data up to a point that meets its recovery requirements. In case of a disruptive incident, the organization can restore its systems and data to a state that is consistent with the defined RPO.
