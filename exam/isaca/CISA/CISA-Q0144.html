Selected Answer: C
Functional or message acknowledgments are a method used to ensure the successful and reliable delivery of messages in an e-commerce environment. When two systems or parties communicate with each other, the receiving party sends an acknowledgment back to the sending party to confirm that the message was received and processed successfully. By using functional or message acknowledgments, the sender can have assurance that the message reached its intended destination and was successfully processed. In case of any communication failure or error, the sender can be notified and take appropriate actions to rectify the issue or resend the message.
