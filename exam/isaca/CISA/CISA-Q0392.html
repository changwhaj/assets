Selected Answer: A
A. performing a business impact analysis (BIA) would be the BEST way to understand the relationships between critical systems. A BIA provides a comprehensive understanding of an organization's critical systems, their interdependencies, and the potential impacts of their failure. By conducting a BIA, an organization can identify critical business processes and the systems and data that support them, and determine the order in which these systems and processes should be restored in the event of a disruption. This information is critical for developing effective business continuity and disaster recovery plans.
