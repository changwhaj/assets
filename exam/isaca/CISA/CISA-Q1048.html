Selected Answer: C
Rapid elasticity is the feature associated with an Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) cloud service provider that allows for the provisioning of new servers as demand changes. Rapid elasticity refers to the ability to quickly and easily scale computing resources up or down based on demand. This allows users to dynamically allocate and deallocate resources as needed, providing flexibility and efficiency in handling varying workloads.
