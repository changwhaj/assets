Adopting an internationally recognized IT governance framework provides a common language and set of practices that are widely accepted across various industries and organizations. This facilitates alignment with industry best practices and enhances communication and collaboration between different business units and support functions. It also allows organizations to leverage readily available resources, methodologies, and frameworks that have gained global acceptance, reducing the need for custom, trial-and-error approaches.
