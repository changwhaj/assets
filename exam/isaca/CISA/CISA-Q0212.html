most effective defense against malicious insiders compromising confidential information is role-based access control. This approach limits access to sensitive information and systems based on an individual's job function or role within the organization. This ensures that only authorized personnel have access to the information they need to perform their job, and reduces the risk of accidental or malicious data breaches. By implementing role-based access control, an organization can reduce the risk of malicious insiders compromising confidential information, by ensuring that the employees have access only to the information they need to do their job.
