A. Communication plan When creating an incident response plan, it's important to include a communication plan during the preparation phase of the plan's life cycle. A communication plan outlines how the organization will communicate during an incident, who will be responsible for communicating, and what information will be communicated to different stakeholders. This is important to ensure that all stakeholders, including employees, customers, partners, and regulators, are informed and kept up-to-date on the incident and its impact. This can help to minimize the potential damage to the organization's reputation, and ensure that the incident is handled in a transparent and timely manner.
