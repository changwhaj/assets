D. Leveraging an IT governance framework Leveraging an IT governance framework is the option that BEST enables alignment of IT with business objectives. IT governance frameworks, such as COBIT or ITIL, provide a structured approach to aligning IT activities and investments with an organization's business objectives. These frameworks help ensure that IT resources are used efficiently, risks are managed effectively, and IT initiatives support the overall goals and strategies of the business.
