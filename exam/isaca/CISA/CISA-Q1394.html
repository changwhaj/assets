Selected Answer: C
When consolidating several applications onto a new server, the greatest concern is the risk that the new server will not be fully utilized after migration (C). This is because underutilization of the new server means that the investment in hardware is not being maximized, leading to inefficiencies in resource usage and potentially higher costs in the long run.
