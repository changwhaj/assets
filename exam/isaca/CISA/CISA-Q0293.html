The correct answer is C : By randomly selecting a sample of new users based solely on user access to all servers in the environment, the bot may not be capturing all relevant data for the audit. This may result in a biased sample that does not accurately represent the entire population of new users. Without maintaining evidence of population completeness, the auditor cannot be confident that the results of the audit are representative and reliable.
