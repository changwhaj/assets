A data analytics team has developed a process automation bot for internal audit that scans user access to all servers in the environment and then randomly selects a sample of new users for testing. Which of the following presents the GREATEST concern with this approach?

  • A. The bot can only select samples from the current period.
  • B. Auditor judgment is removed from the process.
  • C. Evidence of population completeness is not maintained. Most Voted
  • D. Data must be validated manually before being loaded into the bot.

Selected Answer: C
The correct answer is C : By randomly selecting a sample of new users based solely on user access to all servers in the environment, the bot may not be capturing all relevant data for the audit. This may result in a biased sample that does not accurately represent the entire population of new users. Without maintaining evidence of population completeness, the auditor cannot be confident that the results of the audit are representative and reliable.
