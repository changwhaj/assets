The IS auditor should review the cost-benefit documentation to ensure that it is reasonable, accurate, and provides a sound basis for decision-making. This involves assessing the validity of cost estimates, evaluating projected benefits, and ensuring that the financial analysis aligns with industry standards and organizational goals.
