The greatest benefit of integrating information security program requirements into vendor management is the ability to reduce risk in the supply chain. This is because vendor management involves assessing and managing risks associated with third-party suppliers and ensuring that they meet the organization's security requirements

The correct answer is C- Reducing risk in the supply chain. If you look at the CISM material it is all about risk and this is the answer that has anything to do with risk. Rationale: (A) meeting an industry compliance is great, but compliance for compliance sake is not the goal (B) SLA are target objectives which is great for vendor management, but it's not the most relevant to security (C) Reducing Risk is the greatest benefit and most relevant to security (D) vendor performance is great, but if we wanted it to be measurable we would have gone with (B) setting an SLA.
