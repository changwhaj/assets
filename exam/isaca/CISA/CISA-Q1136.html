  • A. Professionalism
  • B. Audit efficiency
  • C. Audit transparency
  • D. Data confidentiality

C. Audit transparency is the PRIMARY advantage of an IS auditor requesting direct access to data required to perform audit procedures. It reduces reliance on potentially biased or filtered information provided by management, contributing to the credibility and objectivity of the audit.
