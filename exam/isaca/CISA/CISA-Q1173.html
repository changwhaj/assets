A risk and controls matrix is a structured document that outlines the specific risks and associated control measures for a particular process or system. In the context of understanding shared control requirements between multiple cloud service providers and the customer organization, a risk and controls matrix can provide a clear and detailed overview of the responsibilities and actions that each party (the cloud service providers and the customer organization) must take to ensure the security and compliance of the cloud environment. This matrix helps in defining and documenting roles, responsibilities, and control measures for all parties involved. It allows the IS auditor to have a comprehensive understanding of the shared control requirements and ensures that there is clarity regarding who is responsible for what aspects of security and compliance in a multi-cloud environment. This is particularly important in a shared responsibility model where both the cloud service providers and the customer organization have their respective responsibilities for security and compliance.
