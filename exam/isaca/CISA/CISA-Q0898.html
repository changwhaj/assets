Selected Answer: A
Capacity management is the process of ensuring that IT resources, including hardware, software, and personnel, are utilized efficiently to meet current and future business needs. The primary objective of capacity management is to ensure that the IT infrastructure can meet the current and future demands of the business while minimizing costs and maximizing efficiency.

A. available resources are used efficiently and effectively Capacity management tools are primarily used to ensure that available resources within an organization's IT infrastructure are utilized efficiently and effectively. These tools help monitor and manage the allocation of resources such as CPU, memory, storage, and network bandwidth to ensure that they are utilized optimally to meet the organization's needs while avoiding over-provisioning or under-provisioning of resources. The goal is to ensure that the IT infrastructure operates efficiently, performs well, and meets the demands of users and applications without unnecessary waste or bottlenecks. While the other options (B, C, and D) can be related to capacity management to some extent, they are not the primary focus of capacity management tools.
