Cultural differences in attitudes towards privacy and security can have a significant impact on the success of the program. Understanding and addressing these cultural differences is crucial to the success of the program.

The MOST important consideration for a global organization that is designing an information security awareness program is cultural backgrounds. A successful security awareness program must be tailored to the cultural context of the organization and the specific needs of its employees. This includes taking into account differences in language, values, beliefs, and attitudes towards security, as well as other cultural factors that can influence the effectiveness of the program. While national regulations, program costs, and local languages are important factors to consider, they are secondary to the cultural context of the organization.
