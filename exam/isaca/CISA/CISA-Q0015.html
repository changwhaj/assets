  • A. Leverage the work performed by external audit for the internal audit testing. Most Voted
  • B. Ensure both the internal and external auditors perform the work simultaneously.
  • C. Roll forward the general controls audit to the subsequent audit year.
  • D. Request that the external audit team leverage the internal audit work. Most Voted
