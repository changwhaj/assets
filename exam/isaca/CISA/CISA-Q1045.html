An IS auditor is performing a follow-up audit and notes that some critical deficiencies have not been addressed. The auditor's BEST course of action is to:

  • A. document management's reasons for not addressing deficiencies.
  • B. postpone the audit until the deficiencies are addressed.
  • C. provide new recommendations.
  • D. assess the impact of not addressing deficiencies.

IS 감사인의 최선의 조치는 경영진이 결함을 해결하지 못한 이유를 문서화하는 것입니다. 이 문서는 여러 가지 이유로 중요합니다. 투명성: 경영진의 이유를 문서화하면 아직 해결되지 않은 문제에 대한 투명성과 명확성을 제공합니다. 의사소통: 이는 감사자와 경영진 간의 의사소통을 촉진하여 특정 결함을 해결하지 않기로 한 결정의 근거를 이해하는 데 도움이 됩니다. 감사 추적: 감사자가 식별된 결함을 추적하고 경영진의 응답을 문서화했음을 보여주는 감사 추적을 생성합니다.

Selected Answer: A
The IS auditor's BEST course of action is to document management's reasons for not addressing deficiencies. This documentation is important for several reasons: Transparency: Documenting management's reasons provides transparency and clarity about the issues that have not been addressed. Communication: It facilitates communication between the auditor and management, helping to understand the rationale behind the decision not to address certain deficiencies. Audit Trail: It creates an audit trail, demonstrating that the auditor has followed up on the identified deficiencies and has documented the responses from management.

Selected Answer: D
D. assess the impact of not addressing deficiencies. In this situation, the IS auditor's best course of action is to assess the impact of not addressing the critical deficiencies. This assessment will help in understanding the potential risks and consequences of not addressing these issues and can provide valuable information to management for decision-making. It may also lead to documenting management's reasons for not addressing the deficiencies and potentially providing new recommendations based on the assessment findings. Postponing the audit may not be the most practical option, as it may not necessarily prompt immediate action on the deficiencies.
