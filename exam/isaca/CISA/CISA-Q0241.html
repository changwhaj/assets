C. To obtain an independent view of vulnerabilities is the PRIMARY reason that an information security manager would contract with an external provider to perform penetration testing. Penetration testing is used to identify vulnerabilities and weaknesses in an organization's information systems, network infrastructure, and applications. Hiring an external provider can provide an unbiased view of the security posture of the organization and offer recommendations for mitigating vulnerabilities.
