It is Pilot Operation. Pilot operation is a method of transitioning to a new system in which the new system is implemented on a small scale to a select group of users or in a limited area. This method helps to reduce the risk associated with the transition by allowing the organization to test the new system in a controlled environment and identify any potential issues or problems before a full implementation. Pilot operation also provides an opportunity to train users on the new system, identify any potential training gaps, and ensure that users are comfortable with the new system before a full implementation.
