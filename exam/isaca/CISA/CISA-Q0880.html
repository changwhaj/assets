Selected Answer: A
A. Greater consistency across the organization: Likely correct. A top-down approach often involves centralized decision-making and enforcement of policies, which can lead to greater consistency in how IT policies are applied across the organization. The most likely outcome of shifting to a top-down approach is greater consistency across the organization (Option A). However, it's important to note that the effectiveness of this shift will depend on how well the top-down approach is implemented and the quality of the policies that are developed and enforced.
