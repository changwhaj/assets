계획된 IT 예산과 조직의 목표 및 전략적 목표의 조정을 검토하는 IS 감사자에게 가장 유용한 옵션은 엔터프라이즈 아키텍처(EA)입니다. 엔터프라이즈 아키텍처는 목표, 목적, 프로세스, 시스템 및 기술 인프라를 포함하여 조직의 IT 환경에 대한 전체적인 보기를 제공합니다. Selected Answer: A
The most helpful option for an IS auditor reviewing the alignment of a planned IT budget with an organization's goals and strategic objectives would be Enterprise Architecture (EA). Enterprise Architecture provides a holistic view of an organization's IT landscape, including its goals, objectives, processes, systems, and technology infrastructure. By analyzing the enterprise architecture, an IS auditor can assess how the planned IT budget aligns with the organization's strategic objectives. They can identify areas where the budget allocation supports or deviates from the goals and make recommendations for adjustments if necessary. While audit recommendations, risk assessment reports, and business impact analysis (BIA) are also valuable in the audit process, they are more focused on specific areas of audit scope rather than providing a comprehensive view of the IT budget alignment with the organization's goals and strategic objectives.
