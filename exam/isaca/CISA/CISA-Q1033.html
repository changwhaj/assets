Selected Answer: D
Before the release of a new application into an organization's production environment, having a Change Approval Board in place is important to ensure that proper testing has occurred and rollback plans are in place. The CAB is responsible for reviewing and approving changes to the IT environment, including new applications or updates, to ensure that they meet quality standards and do not adversely impact the production environment.
