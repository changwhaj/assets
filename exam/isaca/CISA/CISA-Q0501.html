Selected Answer: D
Having all inputs and outputs for potential actions included in the interface control documents is considered the best indication of their completeness because it means that all necessary information required for the interfaces has been documented and accounted for. This reduces the likelihood of missing information or unexpected errors during the development and implementation of the new application. In other words, having a comprehensive and complete list of inputs and outputs helps ensure that the interface design is complete and accurately reflects the requirements and objectives of the application.
