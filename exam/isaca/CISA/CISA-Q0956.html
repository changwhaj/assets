The right answer is B: Because Pre-approval of entry requests involves having visitors request access in advance, which allows the organization to verify their identities and determine if they meet the necessary criteria for entry. This process provides an opportunity for security personnel to perform background checks, review the purpose of the visit, and ensure that only authorized individuals are granted access. Pre-approval helps minimize the risk of unauthorized visitors entering the data center. And for C. Visitors are escorted by an authorized employee: Having authorized employees escort visitors is an additional security measure that can enhance control over visitor access. However, this approach may be resource-intensive, requiring staff members to be available at all times to escort visitors. It may not be practical or scalable for large data centers with a high volume of visitor traffic.
