Selected Answer: D
D. The incident response team has the appropriate training. Ensuring that incident management plans are executed effectively is crucial to minimize the impact of a security incident. One of the most important factors to consider is that the incident response team has the appropriate training. This includes training on incident response procedures and best practices, as well as training on the specific tools and technologies that will be used to respond to incidents. This will ensure that the team is properly prepared to handle the incident, and that they are able to respond quickly and effectively. Training also helps to build the team's confidence and expertise, which can be critical in high-pressure incident response situations. Management support and approval, conducting incident response maturity assessment, and engaging with a reputable managed security services provider are all important, but without the appropriate training, incident response team may not be able to execute the incident management plans effectively.
