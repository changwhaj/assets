A month after a company purchased and implemented system and performance monitoring software, reports were too large and therefore were not reviewed or acted upon. The MOST effective plan of action would be to:

  • A. evaluate replacement systems and performance monitoring software.
  • B. use analytical tools to produce exception reports from the system and performance monitoring software.
  • C. re-install the system and performance monitoring software.
  • D. restrict functionality of system monitoring software to security-related events.

Using analytical tools to produce exception reports allows for the identification and extraction of critical information or anomalies from the large volume of data generated by the monitoring software. Exception reports focus on highlighting deviations, thresholds breaches, or significant events that require immediate attention or action. By implementing exception reporting, the company can filter out non-essential information and focus on the most important and relevant data points. This helps address the problem of large reports overwhelming the review process and enables timely and effective monitoring of critical events or issues. - ChatGPT and it makes a lot of sense
