In the context of IT governance as per ISACA (the organization that offers the CISA certification), ensuring independence from production processes is a key principle. IT governance should provide oversight, direction and control, and should not be directly involved in operational or production activities. This independence allows IT governance to objectively evaluate the effectiveness and efficiency of IT processes, manage risks, and ensure alignment with business objectives. Although sharing IT strategies and creating a roadmap for business gains are also important, independence from production processes is a more fundamental requirement for effective IT governance according to ISACA's framework. Therefore, the answer D is more suitable when considering the CISA exam materials.
