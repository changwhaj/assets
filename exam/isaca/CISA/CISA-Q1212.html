A. during fieldwork. During fieldwork, auditors should communicate their observations to the auditee as they are identified. This allows for timely discussion, clarification, and resolution of issues, ensuring that corrective actions can be taken promptly if needed. Waiting until the end of fieldwork or until the audit report is drafted may delay addressing important issues and could potentially hinder the effectiveness of the audit process. Therefore, it is best practice to communicate audit observations to the auditee as they arise during fieldwork.
