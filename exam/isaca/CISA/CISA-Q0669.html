Facilitating audit risk identification and evaluation workshops: Facilitating audit risk identification and evaluation workshops is an effective way for the audit team to leverage the organization's mature risk management practices. In these workshops, the audit team can collaborate with the organization's management and stakeholders to identify, assess, and prioritize risks. This process can help the audit team gain insights into the organization's risk management practices, identify gaps, and recommend improvements.
