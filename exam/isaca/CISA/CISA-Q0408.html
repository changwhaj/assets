  • B. Establishing a strong ongoing risk monitoring process
  • C. Presenting the risk profile for approval by the risk owner
  • D. Updating the information security standards to include the accepted risk

Selected Answer: B
B. Establishing a strong ongoing risk monitoring process. The primary focus of the information security manager in this situation should be to establish a robust ongoing risk monitoring process to ensure that the risk is being managed effectively and that any issues or concerns are identified and addressed in a timely manner. This will allow the manager to ensure that the risk owner is aware of any potential issues and can take appropriate action to mitigate or manage the risk as necessary.
