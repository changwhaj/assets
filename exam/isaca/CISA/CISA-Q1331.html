A security administrator is called in the middle of the night by the on-call programmer. A number of programs have failed, and the programmer has asked for access to the live system. What is the BEST course of action?

  • A. Review activity logs the following day and investigate any suspicious activity.
  • B. Give the programmer read-only access to investigate the problem.
  • C. Require that a change request be completed and approved.
  • D. Give the programmer an emergency ID for temporary access and review the activity.

이 대답은 실패한 프로그램이 중요한 응용 프로그램인지, 한밤중에 서비스가 필요한지 여부에 따라 크게 달라집니다. 특히 적절한 프로세스와 문서 없이 한밤중에 라이브 시스템에 직접 액세스 권한을 부여하면 심각한 보안 위험이 발생할 수 있으며 승인되지 않은 작업이나 의도하지 않은 결과가 발생할 가능성이 높아질 수 있습니다. 따라서 변경 요청을 완료하고 승인하도록 요구하는 것이 최선의 조치입니다. 이 위험이 높고 위험 등록부에 문서화되어 있는 경우 따라야 할 위험 대응 프로세스가 있어야 합니다.

Selected Answer: D
This answer very much depends on if the failed programs are critical application and that if it need to be in service in the middle of the night. Granting direct access to the live system, especially in the middle of the night without proper process and documentation, can pose significant security risks and increase the potential for unauthorized actions or unintended consequences. Therefore, requiring a change request to be completed and approved is the best course of action. If this risk is high and it is being documented in risk register, then there must be risk response process to follow.
