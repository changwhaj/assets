  • D. Give the programmer an emergency ID for temporary access and review the activity.

Selected Answer: D
This answer very much depends on if the failed programs are critical application and that if it need to be in service in the middle of the night. Granting direct access to the live system, especially in the middle of the night without proper process and documentation, can pose significant security risks and increase the potential for unauthorized actions or unintended consequences. Therefore, requiring a change request to be completed and approved is the best course of action. If this risk is high and it is being documented in risk register, then there must be risk response process to follow.
