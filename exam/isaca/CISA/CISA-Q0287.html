  • A. 재고 데이터는 기업 인트라넷에서 이용 및 다운로드할 수 있습니다.

Topic #: 1

An organization maintains an inventory of the IT applications used by its staff. Which of the following would pose the GREATEST concern with regard to the quality of the inventory data?

  • A. Inventory data is available on and downloadable from the corporate intranet.
  • B. The inventory does not contain a formal risk ranking for all the IT applications.
  • C. The application owner and contact information fields are not required to be completed.
  • D. The organization has not established a formal recertification process for the inventory data.

Selected Answer: C
Yes is C Because Application owner and contact information is critical in identifying who is responsible for the IT applications, how to contact them, and what business processes they support. If these fields are not required to be completed, it is likely that there will be incomplete and inaccurate information about the ownership and usage of the applications in the inventory. This could result in challenges in terms of maintaining and supporting the applications, as well as making informed decisions about future investments and risks.
