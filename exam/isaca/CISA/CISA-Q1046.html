Selected Answer: A
The BEST way to help ensure new IT implementations align with enterprise architecture (EA) principles and requirements is to consider stakeholder concerns when defining the EA. This involves actively involving key stakeholders in the development of the enterprise architecture, ensuring that their requirements, concerns, and perspectives are taken into account. Enterprise architecture is a holistic approach to aligning an organization's business processes, IT infrastructure, and technology solutions with its strategic objectives. By considering stakeholder concerns during the definition of the EA, organizations can better capture the diverse needs and expectations of different business units and ensure that the EA reflects the broader organizational context.
