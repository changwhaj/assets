The PRIMARY objective of implementing IT governance is: C. Value delivery IT governance is primarily focused on ensuring that IT investments and activities deliver value to the organization. While resource management, performance measurement, and strategic planning are important components of IT governance, they are all means to the end of achieving value delivery. Effective IT governance helps organizations make informed decisions about their IT resources, measure performance to ensure alignment with objectives, and plan strategically to maximize value creation.
