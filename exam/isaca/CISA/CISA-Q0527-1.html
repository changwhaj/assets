Its C: When performing a control self-assessment (CSA) for an organization with a large number of widely dispersed employees, the MOST time and cost-efficient approach would be to use a survey questionnaire. A survey questionnaire is a standardized set of questions that can be distributed to a large number of employees simultaneously, regardless of their location. This approach allows for data collection from a diverse group of employees with different experiences and perspectives. It also enables a more objective and consistent assessment of the control environment, as each respondent will provide answers based on the same questions.
