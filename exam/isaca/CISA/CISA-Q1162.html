Virtualization allows for quicker recovery times in the event of a disaster because virtual machines can be easily migrated, replicated, and brought back online faster than traditional physical servers. This reduction in RTO is a significant advantage in disaster recovery scenarios. Options B, C, and D may also have benefits, but they are not as significant as the reduction in RTO provided by virtualization.
