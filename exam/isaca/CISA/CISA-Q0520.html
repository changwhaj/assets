Selected Answer: A
A. Implementing audit logging on systems is the best way to mitigate the risk to the organization. Audit logging is the process of recording system events and user activities, such as login attempts, file access and data modification. This provides a detailed record of who did what and when in the system, which can be used to identify and investigate security incidents, as well as to demonstrate compliance with regulations and policies.
