The lack of acceptance criteria behind user requirements: Acceptance criteria provide a clear definition of what is expected of the software being developed, and they are used to determine whether the software is ready for release. If acceptance criteria are missing, it can lead to misunderstandings between the developers and the users, resulting in a software product that does not meet the users' needs.
