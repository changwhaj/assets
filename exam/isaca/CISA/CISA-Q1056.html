Selected Answer: C
C. Customer satisfaction ratings Customer satisfaction ratings provide direct feedback from the users of the help desk services, indicating how satisfied they are with the quality of service they receive. It measures the end-users' perception of the service provider's performance, which is a crucial aspect of service quality. Higher customer satisfaction ratings generally indicate that the help desk is meeting the needs and expectations of its users effectively.
