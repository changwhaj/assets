Selected Answer: A
Answer is A Of the options listed, the finding that should be of MOST concern to an IS auditor assessing agile software development practices is A. There is a low acceptance rate by the business of delivered software. One of the key principles of agile software development is to prioritize customer satisfaction through early and continuous delivery of working software. If the business is not accepting the delivered software, it indicates a potential misalignment between the development team and the business. This could be due to a variety of reasons, such as a lack of communication or collaboration, unclear requirements, or insufficient testing.
