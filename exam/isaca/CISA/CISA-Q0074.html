A. The system does not have a maintenance plan. During a post-implementation review, an IS auditor should be most concerned with the absence of a maintenance plan for the system. A well-designed maintenance plan is crucial to ensuring that the system continues to function effectively and meet the organization's needs over time. The maintenance plan should include regular updates, monitoring, and testing to identify and resolve any issues that arise. Without a maintenance plan, the system may become vulnerable to security threats or may not perform as intended, which could lead to disruptions in business operations or negative impacts on the organization's reputation.
