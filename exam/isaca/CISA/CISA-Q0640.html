Selected Answer: A
The Correct answer is (A) to Perform a cost-benefit analysis because you need to know if the benefits outweigh the cost of implementing it. Rationale: B. Collect additional metrics - collecting more information does nothing for helping in the decision-making process. In fact it makes it worse because that is just more data to review. While a cost-benefit analysis already has that result for you. C. Begin due diligence on the outsourcing company is incorrect cause it may be too early in the process to do this. Due diligence requires people and time. While a cost-benefit analysis is faster and cheaper way to get the results while due diligence can validate that after the fact. (i.e. from cheap to expensive validation if the effort is warranted.) D. Submit funding requests to senior management is incorrect cause this is premature without due diligence or cost benefit analysis.
