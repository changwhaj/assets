Preserving the same data structure is the most helpful action to ensure the integrity of the system throughout the change. Data structure includes the organization of data, its relationships, and constraints applied to the data. By maintaining the same data structure, the risk of data corruption, loss, or inconsistency is minimized during the transition from one DBMS to another.
