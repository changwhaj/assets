B. Inadequate deliverables When business owners are removed from the project initiation phase, it can lead to a lack of alignment between the project's objectives and the actual needs of the business. This misalignment can result in inadequate deliverables, as the project may not address the essential requirements and objectives of the organization. Without the involvement of business owners, there is a higher risk that the project may produce outputs that do not meet the business's needs, which can be a significant concern for the organization. While incomplete requirements (A), unclear benefits (C), and unrealistic milestones (D) are also important concerns, inadequate deliverables can have a more direct and immediate impact on the project's success and its ability to deliver value to the organization.
