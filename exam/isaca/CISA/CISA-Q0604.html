An audit trail is a record of system activities that is used to facilitate the reconstruction, review, and examination of the sequence of activities surrounding or leading to a specific operation, event, or problem. Ensuring that an application's audit trail has adequate security is crucial. This involves safeguarding the audit trail from unauthorized access, modification, or deletion.
