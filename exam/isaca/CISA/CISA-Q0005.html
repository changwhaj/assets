The issue that should be of MOST concern to an IS auditor regarding a data center's closed circuit television (CCTV) surveillance cameras is A. CCTV recordings are not regularly reviewed. Regular review of CCTV recordings is essential for effective security monitoring and incident response. It allows for timely identification of security breaches, suspicious activities, or potential threats to the data center. Without regular review, valuable evidence and insights into security incidents may be missed, making it challenging to detect and respond to security breaches in a timely manner. While the other options (B, C, and D) are also important considerations for CCTV surveillance, the lack of regular review of CCTV recordings poses a higher risk to the overall security posture of the data center.
