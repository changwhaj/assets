Should be A. Problem management is the process of identifying the underlying causes of incidents and finding permanent solutions to prevent them from recurring. By linking incidents to problem management activities, the organization can identify patterns, root causes, and trends that contribute to repeated incidents. This approach allows for a deeper understanding of the underlying issues and enables the implementation of long-term solutions to prevent similar incidents from happening again in the future.
