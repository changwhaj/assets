C. Aligning with the information security strategy Aligning with the information security strategy ensures that your governance framework is in line with the organization's overall goals and objectives related to security. It helps ensure that security measures are integrated into the broader business strategy and that security efforts are focused on addressing the most critical risks and vulnerabilities. While the other factors mentioned (assessing resource availability, assessing the current state of information security, and aligning with industry best practice frameworks) are also important, they should be done in the context of and in alignment with the organization's security strategy.
