Selected Answer: B
Documenting the chain of custody is crucial because it establishes the chronological history of who had possession of the evidence, when they had it, and how it was stored or handled. This documentation is essential for maintaining the integrity and admissibility of evidence in court. It helps ensure that the evidence collected during the incident response process can be used effectively in any civil litigation that may arise from the incident.
