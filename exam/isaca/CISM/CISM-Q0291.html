C. Containerization solution Containerization separates corporate data and applications from the personal data and apps on the mobile device by creating a secure and isolated container. This container ensures that corporate data is protected and can be remotely managed or wiped without affecting the personal data on the device. It offers a strong layer of security for BYOD programs because it effectively separates and protects corporate data while allowing employees to use their personal devices for work.
