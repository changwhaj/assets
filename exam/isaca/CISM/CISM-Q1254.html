Selected Answer: A
Correct answer: A. present an analysis of the cost and benefit of the changes CISM Q&A 9th, S1-141: Cost-benefit analysis measures the cost of a safeguard versus the benefit it provides and includes risk assessment. The cost of a control should not exceed the benefit to be derived from it. The degree of control employed is a matter of good business judgment. Wrong answer: B. discuss the risk and impact of security incidents if not implemented Risk analysis identifies the risk and treatment options. A business impact analysis identifies the impact from the loss of systems or organization functions.
