A. Incidents can escalate into disasters needing proper response Integrating incident response with business continuity recognizes that some incidents, if not properly managed, can escalate into larger-scale disasters that affect the organization's overall operations. By having an integrated approach, an organization can ensure a coordinated and effective response to incidents, helping to prevent them from becoming full-blown disasters and minimizing their impact on business continuity. This approach aims to detect, respond to, and mitigate incidents at an early stage, reducing the likelihood of them evolving into larger-scale disasters.
