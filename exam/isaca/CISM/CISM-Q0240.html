Selected Answer: D
Before moving production data to the cloud, it is crucial to evaluate and understand the data isolation mechanisms provided by the cloud service provider. This includes assessing the provider's security controls, network segmentation, encryption methods, access controls, and overall architecture. The organization must ensure that the cloud environment can provide the necessary level of isolation to protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of the production data.
