Selected Answer: C
During the post incident review, lessons learned and recommendations for improvements are analyzed and documented. Updating processes and plans is not done at this stage. It is during the Planning & Preparation phase, that the recommendations from PIR are evaluated and implemented. Tabletop exercises could be a perfect opportunity to test the recommendations and update accordingly.
