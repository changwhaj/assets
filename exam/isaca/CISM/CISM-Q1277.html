Real world in dealing with classified data or any data, you would consider the classification before anything. For example, if I'm on a Top Secret computer and data has been approved through some organization such as AWS, classification would be considered 20x over. After that, then backup. Heck, what happens if you back it up to an unclassified thumb drive? Just a way to think about this.
