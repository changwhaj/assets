C. The organization's vision and mission While audit and regulatory requirements (A), information security policies (B), and the results of a comprehensive gap analysis (D) are important factors to consider in developing an information security strategy, they should all align with and support the organization's overall vision and mission. The organization's vision and mission define its long-term goals, values, and purpose, and the information security strategy should be designed to help achieve and protect these overarching objectives. Therefore, the organization's vision and mission should serve as the foundational basis for developing an effective information security strategy that aligns with the organization's broader strategic goals.
