A. Key risk indicators (KRIs) are most effective for communicating forward-looking trends within security reporting. KRIs are metrics that measure potential risks and help organizations to anticipate and respond to future security threats. They provide early warning signals that help organizations take proactive measures to prevent or mitigate risks.

A. Key risk indicators (KRI) focus on forward-looking trends in security reporting, they are indicators that focus on predictive or leading factors. KPIs on the other hand are retrospective in nature, meaning they provide insights into how well an organization has performed in the past or is currently performing. They often measure outcomes based on historical data. So since the question is forward looking, the correct answer is definitely A. Key risk indicators (KRIs).
