Selected Answer: A
Security governance provides the overarching framework, structure, and processes for managing and overseeing the organization's information security activities. It involves defining roles and responsibilities, establishing policies and procedures, and ensuring that security efforts align with the organization's strategic objectives. Security governance sets the tone for effective security management and coordination across various functions within the organization. While security policies, metrics, and guidelines are important components of an information security program, security governance is the foundation that ensures a holistic and well-coordinated approach to security.
