Effect of disruptive event - means that event has materialized and we are in incident management process where as part of detection we should identify it and do a quick analysis to determine severity and pass it to containment process to contain it through IMT/Incident handler who will work with stakeholders to further do detailed assessment of the impact and agree on containment with them. Business Impact analysis is potential impact of each type of incident that may occur on the business so that response can be prioritized and planned.
