A newly appointed information security manager of a retailer with multiple stores discovers an HVAC (heating, ventilation, and air conditioning) vendor has remote access to the stores to enable real-time monitoring and equipment diagnostics. Which of the following should be the information security manager's FIRST course of action?

  • A. Disconnect the real-time access.
  • B. Conduct a penetration test of the vendor.
  • C. Review the vendor contract. Most Voted
  • D. Review the vendor's technical security controls.

C. Review the vendor contract. The first step that the information security manager should take is to review the vendor contract, as it will provide important information about the vendor's access rights and responsibilities. The contract should detail what type of access the vendor has, what data they are able to access, and what measures are in place to protect that data. The review of vendor contract will help the manager understand the scope of the vendor's access and the security controls that are in place to protect the organization's data. Based on the findings, further action can be taken such as reviewing vendor's technical security controls, conducting a penetration test or even disconnecting the real-time access.
