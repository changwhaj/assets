Selected Answer: A
The greatest concern to information security management when an organization shares customer information across its globally dispersed branches should be conflicting data protection regulations. Different countries and regions have their own laws and regulations regarding data protection and privacy, and failure to comply with these regulations can result in significant fines and legal penalties. It's important for the organization to ensure that the data is protected and shared in compliance with all relevant laws and regulations, which can be a complex and challenging task. This includes ensuring that the data is only shared with authorized personnel, that it is securely transferred and stored, and that it is deleted when it is no longer needed.
