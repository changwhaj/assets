Selected Answer: D
Having a robust data backup system is crucial in the event of a ransomware attack. Regularly backing up important data and ensuring the backups are secure and up-to-date can enable an organization to recover its systems and files without paying the ransom. This approach not only mitigates the impact of a ransomware attack but also prevents the loss of critical data.
