Selected Answer: D
This question concerns the documentation of the DRP. Therefore, it is very important that DRP documentation is up-to-date, and that is the most important concern. On the other hand, organization-wide DR training, while important, is a lower priority than updating documentation, and without proper documentation, the training itself will not be effective. Therefore, D should be given higher priority.
