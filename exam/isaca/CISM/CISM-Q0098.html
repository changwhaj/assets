Deploying mobile device management (MDM) is the best option to mitigate the increased risk of theft associated with tablets used to store critical business data. MDM solutions provide centralized control and management of mobile devices, including the ability to enforce security policies, track devices, and remotely wipe them if they are lost or stolen. This ensures that even if a tablet is stolen, the organization can remotely erase its data, minimizing the risk of unauthorized access to critical business information.

A mobile device risk assessment is a process of identifying and assessing the risks associated with using mobile devices in the workplace. It can help organizations to identify vulnerabilities in their mobile device security and to develop mitigation strategies. Mobile device management (MDM) is a software solution that allows organizations to manage mobile devices, such as tablets. MDM can be used to configure tablets, deploy security policies, and track tablet usage. However, MDM cannot prevent theft of tablets.
