Selected Answer: C
Tabletop testing of the incident response plan involves simulating various security incidents, including data breaches, to evaluate the effectiveness of the organization's response procedures. This type of exercise helps identify gaps in communication and coordination among different teams, including legal counsel. By conducting tabletop exercises, an organization can ensure that legal counsel is informed and involved in the incident response process in a timely manner, potentially preventing the situation described in the question.
