An information security manager's first priority after a high-profile system has been compromised is to preserve incident-related data. This includes collecting and preserving any data or evidence that may be relevant to the incident, such as log files, network traffic, system images, and other relevant data. This step is crucial in order to conduct a thorough investigation of the incident, understand the cause and extent of the compromise, and help identify any potential suspects.
