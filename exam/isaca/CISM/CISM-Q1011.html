Selected Answer: D
Conducting a business impact analysis (BIA) helps in identifying and evaluating the potential impacts of disasters or disruptive events on the organization's critical business functions and processes. It provides a foundation for developing appropriate response and recovery strategies, identifying necessary resources, and establishing priorities for recovery efforts. The results of the BIA guide the development of the DRP, making it a crucial initial step in the planning process.
