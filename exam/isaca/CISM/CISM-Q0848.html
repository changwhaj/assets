D. Independent compliance audit An independent compliance audit is the most effective method for identifying issues related to noncompliance with legal, regulatory, and contractual requirements. An independent audit is typically conducted by a third-party or internal audit team that has no vested interest in the outcome, ensuring impartiality and accuracy in the assessment. This approach provides a comprehensive and objective evaluation of an organization's compliance with various requirements and can uncover issues that may not be apparent through other methods.
