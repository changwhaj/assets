Selected Answer: C
C: Examples of help desk requests. Including examples of real help desk requests related to security incidents can provide tangible and practical scenarios that users can relate to. This approach helps users understand the types of security issues they may encounter in their day-to-day work and how to respond effectively. It also allows the training program to address specific incidents that have occurred within the organization, making the training more relevant and tailored to the organization's specific security challenges.
