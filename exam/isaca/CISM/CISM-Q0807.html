Establishing an information security strategy committee is the best way to obtain support for a new organization-wide information security program because it brings together key stakeholders from different parts of the organization to help define and implement the program. This helps to ensure that the program aligns with the overall business goals and objectives and that it has the support and commitment of key stakeholders. The committee can also help to communicate the benefits and importance of the program to the wider organization, and ensure that everyone understands their role in supporting and implementing the program.
