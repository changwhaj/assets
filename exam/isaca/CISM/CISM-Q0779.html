C. Complement the organization's detective controls. The main benefit of implementing a data loss prevention (DLP) solution is to complement the organization's detective controls. DLP solutions help prevent unauthorized disclosure of sensitive data by monitoring, detecting, and preventing the unauthorized transfer or access of sensitive information. While they can enhance security measures, they do not solely eliminate the risk of data loss.
