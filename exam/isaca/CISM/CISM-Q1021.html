Selected Answer: C
The primary benefit of an information security awareness training program is to influence human behavior (Option C). These programs are designed to educate and train individuals within an organization about the importance of information security and how to properly handle sensitive data. By increasing awareness and knowledge, the goal is to influence employees' actions and behaviors to ensure they follow security policies and practices, thereby reducing the risk of security incidents. While evaluating organizational security culture (Option A), enforcing security policy (Option B), and defining risk accountability (Option D) are also important aspects, the primary focus of an information security awareness training program is on influencing human behavior.
