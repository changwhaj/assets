The best way to maintain ongoing senior management support for the implementation of a security monitoring tool is option A: Demonstrate return on investment (ROI). Senior management is typically concerned with the financial aspects of any project, so it is important to show them how the implementation of the security monitoring tool will bring value and help protect the organization's assets. By presenting a clear and well-documented analysis of the potential cost savings and benefits that the tool can bring, such as reducing the risk of security breaches and potential financial losses, you can effectively demonstrate the ROI and gain the support of senior management.
