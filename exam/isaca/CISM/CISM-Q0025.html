Selected Answer: C
Clear and understandable information security objectives are crucial for effective communication and alignment with stakeholders. When objectives are transparent and easily comprehensible, they facilitate better understanding and buy-in from various stakeholders, including employees, management, and other relevant parties. Clarity in objectives helps ensure that everyone involved understands the goals and expectations related to information security, fostering a more cohesive and focused security culture within the organization. While the other options (regular reassessment and reporting, approval by the IT governance function, and identification using global security frameworks and standards) are important aspects of managing information security, clarity and understanding by stakeholders are foundational for successful implementation and support.
