Selected Answer: B
A communication plan outlines the strategies, channels, and protocols for sharing information with various stakeholders during an incident or emergency. It helps ensure that the right information reaches the right people at the right time and in the right way, which is crucial for effective incident response. While the other options, such as escalation procedures, disaster recovery policy, and business continuity plan, are important components of incident response, they do not specifically address the issue of determining what information should be shared with different entities.
