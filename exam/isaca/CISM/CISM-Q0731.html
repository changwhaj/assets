Selected Answer: C
C. Improved risk management. One of the desired outcomes of information security governance is improved risk management. Effective information security governance helps organizations to better understand and manage the risks that they face, and to implement appropriate controls and procedures to mitigate those risks. This helps to ensure that the organization's information assets are protected, and that the organization is better positioned to respond to security incidents. Other outcomes of information security governance, such as penetration testing, a maturity model, and business agility, may be important as well, but they are not as directly related to the goal of improved risk management.
