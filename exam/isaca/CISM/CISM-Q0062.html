The escalation process should be designed to ensure that the appropriate stakeholders are notified at the appropriate time, in order to minimize the impact of the incident on the organization. The primary consideration in developing an escalation process for an incident response plan is ensuring that the affected stakeholders are identified and included in the escalation procedures. This involves understanding who needs to be informed, involved, or notified at different stages of the incident response process based on the nature and severity of the incident.
