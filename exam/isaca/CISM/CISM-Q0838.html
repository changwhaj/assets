C. Well-defined and approved controls Well-defined and approved controls provide a structured framework for managing and mitigating risks. These controls help ensure that risks are identified, assessed, and managed in a consistent and systematic manner. Without clear controls in place, it becomes challenging to make informed decisions about risks, and it can lead to uncertainty and potential vulnerabilities in an organization's risk management process.
