Selected Answer: B
Compartmentalization involves dividing a network or system into distinct compartments or segments, each with its own security controls and access restrictions. This approach limits lateral movement for potential attackers and contains the damage if a breach occurs. It can involve techniques such as network segmentation, VLANs (Virtual Local Area Networks), and strict access controls.
