C. To adequately safeguard the business in achieving its mission. While aligning security goals with the information security program (Option A), ensuring the business supports information security goals (Option B), and obtaining management commitment for sustaining the security program (Option D) are important aspects of information security governance, the overarching goal is to ensure that information security measures are in place to protect the business and its mission. This includes safeguarding the organization's assets, data, and operations from security threats and risks.
