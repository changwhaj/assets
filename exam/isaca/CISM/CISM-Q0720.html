C. Maintain multiple offline backups The best way to ensure the capability to restore clean data after a ransomware attack is to maintain multiple offline backups. Seasondream
2023-04-22 05:40
The BEST answer is D. The question asks "BEST way to ensure the capability to restore clean data". Storing offline backups while a good practice to prevent ransomware attacks does not ENSURE or ASSURE you that you have to capability to perform a restore with CLEAN data. It simply means you have the availability to do so, and just because you have offline backups doesn't mean they are clean, depending on when the backups were taken they could be compromised. If you perform integrity checks on backups you are verifying they are clean, that the data has not been changed or corrupted. Using backups to recover from a ransomware attack can only take you so far if you aren't verifying the backup itself is 100% clean and ready to use.
