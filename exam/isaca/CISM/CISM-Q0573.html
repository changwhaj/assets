Selected Answer: D
D. Conducting a red team exercise: A red team exercise goes beyond vulnerability assessments and penetration testing. It simulates a realistic attack scenario, where a team of skilled professionals (the red team) tries to breach your organization's defenses using the same tactics and techniques that real-world attackers might employ. This approach provides a comprehensive assessment of an organization's threat exposure, including its ability to detect, respond to, and mitigate sophisticated attacks. It helps organizations understand their weaknesses in a more holistic and realistic manner, making it the most effective option for assessing threat exposure.
