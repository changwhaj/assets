Selected Answer: A
A. Protection of business value and assets The PRIMARY benefit achieved when an information security governance framework is aligned with corporate governance is the protection of business value and assets. This alignment ensures that information security practices and controls are integrated into the overall governance structure of the organization, helping to safeguard its assets and maintain the value of the business. While the other options (B, C, and D) may also be important outcomes of aligning information security with corporate governance, protecting business value and assets is the most fundamental and critical objective
