C. Establish an acceptable use policy. An acceptable use policy (AUP) is a set of guidelines that outline what is considered acceptable behavior when using the organization's resources, including personal devices that are allowed on the network under the BYOD program. An AUP can include requirements for device security, such as using encryption and passcodes, and can also specify the types of activities that are prohibited on the network, such as downloading pirated content or engaging in illegal activities. By clearly communicating the expectations to the users, it will be easier to ensure that they adhere to the security standards.
