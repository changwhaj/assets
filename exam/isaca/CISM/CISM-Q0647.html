Selected Answer: A
A. Unexpected outcomes may arise in production. Sanitized data used in testing may not accurately represent the real-world data and scenarios that the application will encounter in production. As a result, unexpected outcomes or issues may arise when the application is deployed in a production environment where it interacts with actual, un-sanitized data. This is why it's essential to use realistic and representative data during testing to minimize the risk of unexpected problems in the production environment.
