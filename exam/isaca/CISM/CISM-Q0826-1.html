There is nothing call Mandatory Access Control address. It is Media Access Control address. And yes it will allow the WiFi clients to authenticate with Wireless router based MAC address but it is not secure. The hacker may not need to crack your WPA2 password at all. So go with VPN. This is a daily life example where we use our wireless network at home or remote place to connect to office and tunnel takes care of most of the part. Of course it is better to have a 2 factor authentication on top of it. So go with C.
