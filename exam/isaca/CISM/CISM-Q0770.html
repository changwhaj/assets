The correct answer is D. Containment. During the incident response process, containment is the phase where the information security manager takes steps to prevent the malware from spreading further within the system or network. This involves isolating the affected systems, disconnecting them from the network, and implementing safeguards to prevent the malware from spreading to other systems or users. Once containment is successfully completed, the information security manager can be confident that the malware has not continued to spread.
