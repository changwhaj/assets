right ans is C - Before taking any other steps, it's essential to conduct a risk assessment when implementing a new critical cloud application. Once you understand the risks and requirements, you can then prioritize and plan for actions like developing a training plan (B), performing a skills gap analysis (D), and establishing a capability maturity model (A) to support the application effectively.
