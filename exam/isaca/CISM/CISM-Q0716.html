The MOST critical factor for information security program success is "B. The information security manager's knowledge of the business." The success of an information security program depends on a number of factors, but a strong understanding of the business by the information security manager is critical. This requires the information security manager to have a good understanding of the organization's goals, objectives, operations, and risk profile, and to be able to align the information security program accordingly.
