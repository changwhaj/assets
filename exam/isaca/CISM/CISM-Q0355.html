Selected Answer: D
D. Educating users on social media risks is the best way to minimize the risk of inadvertently disclosing internal business information on social media. DLP solutions can help prevent data loss, limiting access to social media sites can prevent employees from using them, but both of these methods don't prevent the employees to inadvertently disclose internal information. Developing social media guidelines can help employees understand what kind of information is appropriate to share and what isn't, but education is the key. Educating employees on the risks associated with social media use, as well as providing them with guidelines and best practices for using social media in a professional setting, can help reduce the risk of sensitive information being accidentally disclosed.
