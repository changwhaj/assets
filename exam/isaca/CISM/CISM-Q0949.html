Selected Answer: C
C. The root cause was not identified. Identifying the root cause of the ransomware incident is of utmost importance. Understanding how the ransomware entered the organization's systems and what vulnerabilities or weaknesses were exploited is critical for preventing similar incidents in the future. Without identifying the root cause, the organization may remain vulnerable to further attacks, making it the top concern for the information security manager.
