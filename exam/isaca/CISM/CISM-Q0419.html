D. Conduct an impact analysis. The first action that an organization should take to ensure that the risk associated with a potential advanced persistent threat (APT) is appropriately managed is to conduct an impact analysis. This involves assessing the potential impact that a successful APT could have on the organization, including the potential loss of sensitive data, disruption of business operations, and damage to the organization's reputation.
