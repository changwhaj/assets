Selected Answer: A
A. informing management about the security of business operations. Providing regular updates and informing management about the security of business operations is a critical aspect of maintaining their support for the information security program. This involves communicating the effectiveness of security measures, highlighting achievements, and addressing any concerns or risks that may impact the organization. By keeping management informed about the security posture and its positive impact on business operations, it helps build trust and ensures ongoing support. While the other options (B. identifying the risks and consequences, C. benchmarking the security programs, and D. implementing a comprehensive security awareness program) are valuable activities, consistently communicating the security status and its alignment with business objectives is essential for maintaining long-term management support.
