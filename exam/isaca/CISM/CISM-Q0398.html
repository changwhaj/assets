Selected Answer: C
The organization's risk tolerance has the greatest influence on an organization's information security strategy. Risk tolerance is the level of risk an organization is willing to accept or tolerate in pursuit of its objectives. An organization's information security strategy should be designed to manage risks within its risk tolerance. Industry security standards, organizational structure, and information security awareness can also influence an organization's information security strategy, but risk tolerance is the most significant factor.
