Correct answer: D. enable the team to develop effective response interactions CISA Q&A 12th, A4-222: The primary purpose of tabletop testing is to practice proper coordination because it involves all or some of the crisis team members and is focused more than coordination and communication issues than on technical process details. This type of test can identify missing or incorrect procedures because representatives responsible for performing the tasks are present.
