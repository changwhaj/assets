A. 사고 대응팀에 지속적인 교육을 제공하는 것은 새로운 APT(지능형 지속 위협) 공격자로부터 보호하는 가장 좋은 방법입니다. APT 공격자는 정교한 기술을 사용하여 조직의 네트워크를 손상시키고 민감한 데이터를 유출하는 고도로 숙련되고 동기가 부여된 공격자입니다. 이러한 유형의 위협으로부터 효과적으로 보호하려면 APT 공격을 신속하게 탐지, 대응 및 억제할 수 있는 잘 훈련된 사고 대응 팀을 보유하는 것이 중요합니다. 지속적인 교육을 통해 사고 대응 팀은 항상 최신 위협 인텔리전스를 확보하고 APT 공격자를 효과적으로 방어하는 데 필요한 지식과 기술을 갖추게 됩니다.

Selected Answer: D
All of the options listed can be useful in protecting against advanced persistent threats (APTs), but the BEST method to protect against emerging APT actors depends on the specific situation and the organization's security needs. That being said, option D, implementing proactive systems monitoring, is generally considered a critical security measure in protecting against APTs. APT actors are known for their ability to evade detection and persist within a system for long periods of time, making proactive monitoring a key defense. This approach involves monitoring system logs, network traffic, and other sources of information to detect potential threats before they can cause damage.

Selected Answer: A
A. Providing ongoing training to the incident response team is the best method to protect against emerging advanced persistent threat (APT) actors. APT actors are highly skilled and motivated attackers who use sophisticated techniques to compromise organizations' networks and exfiltrate sensitive data. To effectively protect against these types of threats, it's important to have a well-trained incident response team that is able to quickly detect, respond to, and contain APT attacks. Ongoing training ensures that the incident response team is always up-to-date with the latest threat intelligence and has the knowledge and skills needed to effectively defend against APT actors.
