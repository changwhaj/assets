Selected Answer: D
The most immediate concern for an information security manager of an e-commerce business reviewing the results of a business continuity plan (BCP) review should be if the recovery time objective (RTO) was not met during a recent power outage. The recovery time objective (RTO) is the amount of time within which a critical business function must be restored following a disruption. If the RTO was not met, it means that the business continuity plan did not effectively enable the organization to recover critical systems and processes in a timely manner. This could have serious consequences for the organization, such as loss of revenue, reputation damage, or regulatory fines. The other findings may also be important, but they would not have the immediate impact on the organization's ability to continue operating as a missed RTO does.
