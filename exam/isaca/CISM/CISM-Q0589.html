A. ensure the continuity of the organization’s essential services. The primary objective of timely declaring a disaster is to ensure the continuity of the organization's essential services. This means that the organization is able to quickly activate its disaster recovery plan and take the necessary actions to minimize the disruption to its operations. This can include protecting critical physical assets, engaging business management in the recovery process, and assessing and correcting disaster recovery process deficiencies, but all of those are secondary objectives or outcomes.
