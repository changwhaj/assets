The option that BEST facilitates the effectiveness of cybersecurity incident response is C. Increasing communication with all incident response stakeholders. Effective communication is crucial during a cybersecurity incident response as it ensures that all stakeholders, such as IT teams, management, and relevant departments, are aware of the incident and can contribute to its resolution. By increasing communication, teams can share information, coordinate their efforts, and make informed decisions to mitigate the impact of the incident. This includes sharing updates on the incident's progress, identifying potential vulnerabilities, and implementing necessary security measures.
