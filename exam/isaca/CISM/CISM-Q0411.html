D. The response team is trained on the plan. The effective execution of an incident response plan relies heavily on the preparedness and capabilities of the response team. Training the response team on the plan is critical because it ensures that team members are familiar with their roles and responsibilities, as well as the procedures outlined in the plan. This familiarity with the plan enables a more efficient and coordinated response to security incidents, reducing the potential impact and damage caused by the incident.
