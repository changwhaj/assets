B. Information assets affected. Classifying incidents based on the information assets affected helps prioritize incidents according to their potential impact on the organization's data and operations. This allows for a more targeted and effective incident response, ensuring that critical assets are protected and restored in a timely manner. While considering root causes, recovery point objectives (RPOs), and estimated time to recover are important aspects of incident management, they are usually secondary to understanding the impact on information assets when determining the escalation process.
