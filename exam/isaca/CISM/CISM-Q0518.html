The best reason to consolidate security operations teams across a global organization would be B. Enhanced visibility of threats. By consolidating the security operations teams, organizations can centralize their monitoring, analysis, and response capabilities. This allows for better coordination and sharing of information, leading to improved visibility into potential threats across the entire organization. With enhanced visibility, organizations can more effectively detect and respond to security incidents, ultimately reducing the risk of cyber attacks. While compliance with regulatory requirements, detection of fraud, and cost reduction are important considerations, enhanced threat visibility is crucial for maintaining a strong cybersecurity posture.
