Performing frequent security reviews of the audit log The most effective way to help assure the integrity of an organization's accounting system is by performing frequent security reviews of the audit log. Monitoring the audit log helps in identifying and addressing any suspicious or unauthorized activities that may compromise the integrity of the accounting system. It allows organizations to detect anomalies and potential security breaches in real-time or shortly after they occur, helping to maintain the accuracy and reliability of financial data.
