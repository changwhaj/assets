Selected Answer: C
C. Identifying which business functions are critical to the organization. Before you can determine recovery time objectives (RTOs), minimum resources needed for recovery, or interdependencies among critical functions, you need to first identify which business functions are critical to the organization. This involves assessing and prioritizing the various functions and processes within the organization to determine which ones are essential for its continued operation. Once you've identified the critical functions, you can then move on to the other steps, such as determining RTOs and resource requirements.
