A. Potential business loss. When determining asset valuation, it is crucial to consider the potential business loss associated with the asset. The potential business loss refers to the impact or consequences that would arise if the asset were compromised, damaged, or lost. By evaluating the potential business loss, organizations can prioritize their investments in protecting and securing assets based on their criticality and the potential impact on business operations, reputation, and financial stability.
