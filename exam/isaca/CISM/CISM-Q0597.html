Selected Answer: C
C. Data for the organization to assess progress The MAIN outcome from monitoring key performance indicators (KPIs) for a corporate security management program should be to provide data for the organization to assess its progress. Monitoring KPIs allows the organization to track the effectiveness of its security measures, identify areas that may need improvement, and make informed decisions to enhance security. While the other options (A, B, and D) can be important components of a security management program, they are not the primary outcome of monitoring KPIs.
