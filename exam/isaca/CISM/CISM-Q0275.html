The correct answer is B. Decreased number of incidents that have occurred. Explanation: Among the options provided, the decreased number of incidents that have occurred is the best demonstration that an anti-phishing campaign is effective. Here's why this option is the best choice: B. Decreased number of incidents that have occurred: The primary goal of an anti-phishing campaign is to reduce the number of successful phishing attacks and incidents. A decrease in the number of incidents indicates that the campaign's educational efforts and awareness training are helping employees recognize and avoid phishing attempts. C. Decreased number of phishing emails received: While a decrease in the number of phishing emails received is positive, it may not solely indicate that the campaign is effective, as attackers can change tactics.
