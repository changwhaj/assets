Selected Answer: C
The MOST important to include in an information security policy is C. Best practices. While all the options listed can be relevant to an information security policy, best practices play a crucial role in ensuring the security of information assets. Best practices refer to industry-recognized guidelines, standards, and procedures that are considered effective in protecting information and mitigating risks. Including best practices in an information security policy helps establish a baseline for security measures and provides clear guidance on the recommended actions and controls that should be implemented. It ensures that the organization follows proven methods and approaches to safeguard its information assets.
