A. Identify critical business processes. The first step in developing a business continuity plan (BCP) is to identify critical business processes. This step involves conducting a thorough assessment of your organization's operations to determine which processes are essential for its continued functioning. Once you have identified these critical processes, you can then proceed to determine the business recovery strategy, available resources, and other elements of the BCP.
