There is a difference between incident management and incident response. Incident management is actions taken prior to, during and after the incident. This includes pro-active and reactive detection of incidents. Incident response is actions taken when an incident has been declared, i.e. after detection and reporting. Among verification, assigning ownership, and triage this also includes corrective actions like containment, eradication and recovery. Therefore, the most likely answer is C, corrective.
