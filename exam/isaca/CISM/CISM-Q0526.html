Selected Answer: A
A. Validate the authenticity of the patch should be the first step in patch management procedures when receiving an emergency security patch. Before installing any patch, it is important to ensure that it is genuine and has been released by a trusted source. This step will help to prevent the installation of malicious software or unauthorized patches that could have negative consequences on the system. This step can be done by checking the software vendor's website or by contacting them directly to confirm the authenticity of the patch. After the authenticity of the patch is confirmed, then it is important to schedule patching based on the criticality and conduct comprehensive testing of the patch to ensure that it will not cause any issues on the system before installing it.
