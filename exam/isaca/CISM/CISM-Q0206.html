Management has expressed concerns to the information security manager that shadow IT may be a risk to the organization. What is the FIRST step the information security manager should take?

  • A. Block the end user's ability to use shadow IT
  • B. Update the security policy to address shadow IT
  • C. Determine the value of shadow IT projects
  • D. Determine the extent of shadow IT usage

D. Determine the extent of shadow IT usage. Before taking any action, it's essential to understand the scope and scale of the problem. By determining the extent of shadow IT usage, the information security manager can gather valuable information about which departments or individuals are using unauthorized IT resources, what types of systems or services they are using, and the potential risks involved. This information will help in developing a well-informed strategy to address the issue effectively.
