Selected Answer: B
B. The vendor’s controls are in line with the organization’s security standards. When an organization is considering using a third party to host sensitive archived data, it is important to verify that the vendor's controls are in line with the organization's security standards. This includes reviewing the vendor's security policies, procedures, and technical controls to ensure that they meet or exceed the organization's own standards for data protection. This will help ensure that the organization's sensitive data is protected from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.
