Selected Answer: D
Senior management support for an information security program is best indicated by their active involvement in a steering committee. A steering committee typically consists of top-level executives who provide strategic guidance and oversight for the program. When senior management participates in such a committee, it shows their commitment to the program and their willingness to allocate resources and make decisions to ensure the program's success.
